The TechPort team is a unique group of developers, designers, architects, and engineers whose primary goal is technology communication, via the use of their web-based portal. TechPort gathers technology investment information from across NASA and transforms them into opportunities for collaboration and partnerships, through robust analyses and data visualizations. These both identify key technology drivers that enable Agency decision making and allow the public to discover the technologies NASA is working on every day to explore space, understand the universe, and improve aeronautics. Whether it's a concept in its early stage, a prototype, or a fully developed technology - it's all here in TechPort. We invite you to come look around!
